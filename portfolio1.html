Solution and final application:

Our group is doing drone food delivery, the problem we found through our research and interview is safety. This is because the food may be taken by others since drone may not sure whether is consumer. Moreover, the food may not be fresh when consumer cannot take their food on time. In order to solve this problem, we came out with two pick-up method which are picking up the food from drone directly and picking up the food from locker. Consumer are able to choose their pick-up method after they paying for their order through our APP.
