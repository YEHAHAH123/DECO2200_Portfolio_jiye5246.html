Important design decision:

1. At first, our solution is picking up the food by entering password. After we received some feedback from our users, most of them felt that password is annoying and inconvenient when people entering the wrong password. Moreover, it is also dangerous to enter password on drone. Thus, we changed our idea of password to QR code.

2. For our tracing page, users used to be able to track their order in two ways (real-time tracking and normal tracking) by dragging the button. When we did user testing, we found that many people are confused about that and most of them feel it is useless. Therefore, we changed it. For now, people are able to check the drone and view the list of their order by dragging the button. It is more convenient for people.
